Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TrackHSA, operated by Innovative AI Apps LLC at trackhsaapp.com ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile app and website.

1. Information We Collect or Process

Account Information

  • Email address and unique account identifier when you sign in (Apple/Google).
  • Subscription tier and related billing status metadata.

Receipt Data (Stored On Your Device)

  • Receipt images, OCR text, vendor names, totals, categories, and line items are stored locally on your device by default.
  • These records remain on-device unless you choose to export/share them or enable AI analysis.

AI Analysis Data (Optional)

  • When AI analysis is enabled, extracted receipt text and related receipt fields (for example vendor, totals, category, and line items) may be securely sent for processing.
  • This processing is used only to return structured receipt details to your app.
  • Receipt images are not uploaded as part of this AI text-analysis flow.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Advertising and Diagnostics

  • Free-tier users may see ads through Google AdMob.
  • We may collect limited diagnostics (for example, crash or reliability events) to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate and secure your account.
  • Analyze receipts and organize your records.
  • Provide support and communicate important service updates.
  • Maintain app reliability, abuse prevention, and service integrity.

3. Storage and Security

  • Receipt records are stored on-device in your app sandbox.
  • We currently do not provide cloud backup or multi-device sync for receipts.
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards for data handling.

4. Third-Party Services

TrackHSA uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: supabase.com/privacy
  • OpenAI: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Google AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can delete receipts from within the app.
  • You can request account deletion through in-app settings/support.
  • You can control ad tracking preferences through your device settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

TrackHSA is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date on this page.
